About Shift Predictor

What is Shift Predictor?

Shift Predictor is a tool designed to help individuals working in rotating shifts, like healthcare professionals, factory workers, or anyone who follows regular shift patterns, easily predict their shift for future.

How does it work?

By inputting the shift sequence, the current shift, and the weekly off day, Shift Predictor calculates the user's next shift based on a selected date. It helps plan schedules in advance and avoids confusion.

Features

  • Supports multiple shift sequences
  • Predicts the user's shift based on a selected date
  • Handles weekly off days for accurate predictions
  • Easy-to-use interface with a simple setup
